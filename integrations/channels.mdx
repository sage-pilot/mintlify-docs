Use channel integrations to bring customer conversations into Sagepilot and route them to AI agents, human teams, or both.

## Start here

Open **Channels** in Sagepilot and click **Create Channel**. The setup modal groups channels by the surface they connect:

## Before you connect a channel

- Create or choose the AI agent that should respond on the channel.
- Create teams if escalations should route to a specific team.
- Keep provider credentials ready for channels that require external authentication.
- Use a workspace admin account when connecting OAuth-based channels.
