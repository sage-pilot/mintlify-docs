Use Social engagement to review social conversations that need attention outside the standard support inbox.

## Areas

- **Posts**: review synced posts and comment threads.
- **Stories**: monitor story interactions where supported.
- **X / Twitter**: review monitored X activity and route replies.

## Typical workflow

<Steps>
  <Step title="Connect a social channel">
    Connect Instagram, Facebook Messenger, or X from **Guides > Channel integrations**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review incoming activity">
    Open Social engagement and choose Posts, Stories, or X.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Assign or automate">
    Use the configured social agent and team routing to decide which items need AI or human handling.
  </Step>
</Steps>
