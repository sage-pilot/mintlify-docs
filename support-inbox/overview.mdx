The support inbox is where teams handle day-to-day customer operations.

## Document these workflows

- Reviewing open conversations.
- Using saved views and filters.
- Assigning tickets to people, teams, or AI agents.
- Managing customer context.
- Handling voice calls and linked conversations.
- Reviewing social conversations and backoffice tasks.
