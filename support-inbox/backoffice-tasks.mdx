Backoffice tasks help teams track issues that are related to support work but need operational follow-up.

## Views

- **All Issues**
- **My Issues**
- **Unassigned**
- **High Priority**
- **Overdue**

## Typical workflow

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Backoffice Tasks">
    Use this view when a conversation creates an internal follow-up item.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Assign ownership">
    Assign the task to the right teammate or leave it unassigned for triage.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Track priority and due date">
    Use priority and due-date views to keep operational work from slipping.
  </Step>
</Steps>
