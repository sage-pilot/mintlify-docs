## Where social agents are used

- Instagram comment workflows.
- Facebook Messenger Page comment workflows.
- Social engagement review queues.

## Setup checklist

- Create or choose a social agent.
- Attach the agent to the relevant social channel.
- Review comment reply, hide, and delete settings on the channel.
