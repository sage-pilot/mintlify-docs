Engage is the outbound and lifecycle messaging area in Sagepilot. Use it to define audiences, build content, launch campaigns, automate journeys, and review delivery history.

## Document these workflows

- Campaign setup and delivery history.
- Lists, segments, and audience targeting.
- WhatsApp, email, RCS, and SMS templates.
- Journey automation.
- Marketing AI workflows.
